# Index

Read this first. Load the **spine** always. Pull **ways-of-working** and **preferences** entries only when the *when-this-applies* hook matches the task at hand. This is how the steer stays useful across a long session and across many projects — you carry the small always-on layer and reach for the rest on relevance, instead of ingesting everything and losing half of it.

## Spine — load always

| Topic | Hook |
|---|---|
| [spine/principles.md](spine/principles.md) | Universal engineering principles (DRY, KISS, YAGNI, single responsibility, clarity, security-as-default, document-the-why). Applies to any code, any stack. |
| [spine/conduct.md](spine/conduct.md) | How the agent behaves: verify before claiming done, surface trade-offs, ask before choosing a strategy or changing requirements, no sycophancy, no silent shortcuts. Applies to every turn. |
